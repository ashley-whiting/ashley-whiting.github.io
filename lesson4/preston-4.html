Site Purpose

We can’t control the weather, but we can plan for it! This website’s purpose is to provide predictions of the day’s weather as accurately as possible to local user. The name of the site is My Weather because it only shows weather for the Idaho cities listed on the site. Users will be able to see an hourly and a daily forecast, along with a ‘storm watch’ radar in order to keep an eye on incoming storms.
