YOUR Weather When YOU Need It

Target Audience

The ‘My Weather’ site’s target audience will be the residents of the cities of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. Their occupations will range from farmers, school teachers, commuters on their way to the office, hikers looking for the best trail, etc. Regardless, each person coming to this site will need an accurate weather prediction in order to plan their day’s activities.


Persona 1:

A group of young hikers on spring break from school. Age ranges from 18-30, male and female in good health. Seeking an accurate weather prediction for the next day, specifically around their desired hiking trails. They need to know if weather conditions will be conducive for their 6 hour hike, or if they should plan on hiking another day.

Persona 2:

Personal 2: Middle aged man living in Idaho with a 45-60 min commute via car on busy freeways. It is the middle of winter and he needs to know how much snow tonight will bring and what the road conditions will be like in the morning. He will need to know what time of day will be the safest for his commute, and if there are roads that will be safest to travel on.


  • Scenario 1: What is the high and low temperature tomorrow? Will I need a coat or a light sweater?
  • Scenario 2: How much snow should we expect tonight? Do I need to plan on shoveling my driveway before I head out for work?
  • Scenario 3: Will it rain or be sunny tomorrow? Do I need to send my child to the birthday party with sunscreen or an umbrella?
  • Scenario 4: When is that storm supposed to start? Should I leave work early to beat it?
  • Scenario 5: How long is this drought supposed to last/will it rain tonight? Do I need to water my lawn?
